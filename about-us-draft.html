Troy Schipdam is a Masters candidate in the Library and Information Science program at Pratt University. He is currently employed as a used book buyer at Strand Books, and as a freelance sound designer for live theater projects.

Outside of work, Troy is a writer, with work published in Worthy Tales magazine and online in The Paris Review, among some other places.

Troy is a native New Yorker from Queens, currently living in Crown Heights, Brooklyn.
