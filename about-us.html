About Us | Post-Punk | No Wave | Free Jazz | Minimalism

Troy Schipdam (author of the Free Jazz and Minimalism pages) is a Masters candidate in the Library and Information Science program at Pratt University. He is currently employed as a used book buyer at Strand Books, and as a freelance sound designer for live theater projects.

Outside of work, Troy is a writer, with work published in Worthy Tales magazine and online in The Paris Review, among some other places.

Troy is a native New Yorker from Queens, currently living in Crown Heights, Brooklyn.

Jackson Galati (author of the Post-Punk and No Wave pages) is also a Masters candidate in the Library and Information Science program at the Pratt Institute. Outside of his studies, Jackson volunteers at the Museum of Reclaimed Urban Space and is an avid photographer and musician.

Jackson is a native of the Hudson Valley in New York and currently lives is Bushwick, Brooklyn.
